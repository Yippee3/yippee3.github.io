Join Our Team: Recruitment & Opportunities

We are always seeking highly motivated and talented individuals to join the Neuro-SC Lab. Our work is highly interdisciplinary, combining single-cell technology, neural biology, and advanced bioinformatics.

Postdoctoral Fellows

Candidates should have a strong background in: single-cell omics, neuroscience, computational biology (R/Python), or statistics. Experience with large-scale data analysis is a plus.

Openings: Continuous recruitment

Ph.D. and Master's Students

We welcome applications from both computational and experimental students. Prospective students should be eager to learn single-cell techniques and apply quantitative methods to answer fundamental questions.

Next intake: Fall 2026 application cycle (tentative)
